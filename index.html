About Us

Your Trusted Security Partner Since 2006

17+ Years of Excellence

Established in 2006, Boera Main Security Services Ltd specializes in professional security solutions for public and private institutions across Kenya.

Boera Main Security Services is a trusted security company in Nairobi, dedicated to providing reliable security solutions for individuals, businesses, and communities. As one of the best private security companies in Kenya, we specialize in delivering top-notch services that include professional guards, private security services and advanced security services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our offerings range from residential and corporate security to event security and CCTV installations. With a team of highly trained professionals and cutting-edge technology, we are committed to ensuring your safety and peace of mind.

At Boera Main Security Services, we prioritize integrity, professionalism, and innovation, making us the go-to provider for security guard services and security services in Nairobi.
